# Overview

This workshop enables field teams to **rapidly build customer-specific PoCs** that demonstrate the power of combining unstructured document knowledge with structured enterprise data.

**Go from customer conversation to working demo in under an hour.**

## The Customer Opportunity

Every customer has valuable knowledge spread across:

- **Documents** — Policies, procedures, manuals, FAQs
- **Data Systems** — Databases, data warehouses, business applications

They want a single AI-powered interface where employees can ask questions and get answers from **both sources** — without knowing where the information lives.

## Your Accelerator

This solution accelerator lets you demonstrate exactly that capability, **customized to your customer's industry and use case**:

- **Foundry IQ** — Agentic retrieval over documents (plan, iterate, reflect)
- **Fabric IQ** — Natural language queries over structured data via ontology
- **Multi-Tool Agent** — Combines both to answer complex business questions

!!! success "Why This Matters for PoCs"
    Instead of weeks building a custom demo, generate realistic sample data and a working agent for **any industry** in minutes. Walk into customer meetings with a tailored demo that speaks their language.
